The 2019 South Korean television series Extraordinary You stars Kim Hye-yoon, Rowoon, Lee Jae-wook, Lee Na-eun, Jung Gun-joo, Kim Young-dae, and Lee Tae-ri. The webtoon July Found by Chance, which debuted on Daum Webtoon in January 2018, served as its inspiration. The show premiered on MBC TV from October 2 to November 21, 2019, on Wednesdays and Thursdays at 21:00 (KST).
