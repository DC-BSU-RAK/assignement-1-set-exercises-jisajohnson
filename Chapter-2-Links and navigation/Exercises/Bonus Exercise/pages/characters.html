Characters

  • Eun Dan-Oh:A high school girl with heart disease who found out she lives in a comic world where her fate is predetermined.
  • Number 13/Haru:An extra with no name who plays a huge importance in changing Dan-Oh's fate.
  • Baek Kyung:Dan-Oh's fiance and he was her childhood friend with an egoistic personality. He is also a member of the A3.
  • Oh Nam-Joo:The male lead of the Comic Book "Secret" who saves the female lead whenever she is bullied or comes across any trouble and he is the leader of A3.
  • Yeo Joo-da:The female lead of the Comic Book "Secret" who gets bullied by her classmates for being poor.
  • Lee Do-hwa: Dan-Oh's friend who is very cheerful and like Yeo Joo-da and He is a member of A3.
  • Jinmichae:He is a mysterious cook who works for the high school and know more about the comic world more than anyone.
