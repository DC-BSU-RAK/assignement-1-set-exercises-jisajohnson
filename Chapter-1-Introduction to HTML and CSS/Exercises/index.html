Chilli Chicken

Originally from a Chinese restaurant in India, chilli chicken is an Indian-Chinese cuisine.
It is well-known for having a sweet and spicy flavor that many Indians enjoy.
There are numerous methods to prepare this dish; some people prefer it dry, while others enjoy it with gravy, etc.
This recipe adds more flavor to the main course and might make the perfect side dish for noodles or fried rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g Boneless Chicken
  • Corn Starch
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Egg white
  • Schezwan sauce or Chilli sauce
  • Soya sauce
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Red Kashmiri Chilli Powder or Paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Salt or MSG
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Medium onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Scallions
  • Spring Onions
  • Green Chillies

Method

  1. Slice the boneless Chicken into small pieces.
  2. Mix 1 and a half teaspoon soya sauce, 1 and a half teaspoon Schezwan sauce, a dash of salt and a tad of black pepper into the bowl with the Boneless Chicken.
  3. After mixing, add 2 to 3 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 to 2 tablespoon all purpose flour and 1 egg white.
  4. Mix well, make sure the chicken is coated well, if it seems dry, add a small ounce of water.
  5. Add a tad of Kashmiri Chilli Powder or Paprika so that it gives red colour to the chicken. You can also add food colour instead.
  6. Mix it well, once it is mixed properly. Cover it with a cling film for the mixture to get abosorbed.
  7. Take another bowl, mix 1 tablespoon soya sauce, 2 tablespoon Schezwan sauce, 2 tablespoon tomato ketchup, half teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder, 1 teaspoon vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar and 2 to 3 tablespoon water.
  8. Make sure to prepare these for the sauces, a sliced medium sized onion, 1 tablespoon of chopped garlic, slit 1 to 2 green Chillies half a cup of cubed bell peppers, 1 chopped scallions and for the garnish, 2 tablespoon of spring onions
  9. Take a deep frying pan, add 1 inch of oil in the pan on a medium flame.
  10. When the oil is hot, start adding the battered chicken pieces into the hot oil one by one.
  11. Fry on a medium heat and keep on stirring it until it turns golden and crisp. Make sure it is evenly cooked inside and outside.
  12. Take them out once they are cooked well and place them in a paper napkin so that the excess oil is taken out.
  13. For the sauce, heat the pan with 1 and a half tablespoon of oil on a high flame.
  14. Add the chopped garlic and fry it for 1 minute.
  15. After that, add the sliced onion,green chilly slits, chopped scallions and cubed bell peppers. Fry them until they are slightly tender.
  16. Pour the sauces which created after preparing the battered chicken. Mix it with the veggies and wait till the turn thick.
  17. Add the crisp golden chicken into the sauces and Mix it well.
  18. Once that is done, pour it in a dish and Enjoy! 😋
