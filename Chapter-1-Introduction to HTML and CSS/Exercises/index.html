Chilli Chicken

Originally from a Chinese restaurant in India, chilli chicken is an Indian-Chinese cuisine.
It is well-known for having a sweet and spicy flavor that many Indians enjoy.
There are numerous methods to prepare this dish; some people prefer it dry, while others enjoy it with gravy, etc.
This recipe adds more flavor to the main course and might make the perfect side dish for noodles or fried rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g Boneless Chicken
  • Corn Starch
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Egg white
  • Schezwan sauce or Chilli sauce
  • Soya sauce
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Red Kashmiri Chilli Powder or Paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Salt or MSG
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Medium onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Scallions
  • Green Chillies

Method

  1. Slice the boneless Chicken into small pieces
  2. Mix 1 and a half teaspoon soya sauce, 1 and a half teaspoon Schezwan sauce, a dash of salt and a tad of black pepper into the bowl with the Boneless Chicken
  3. After mixing, add 2 to 3 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 to 2 tablespoon all purpose flour and 1 egg white
  4. Mix well, make sure the chicken is coated well, if it seems dry, add a small ounce of water
  5. Add a tad of Kashmiri Chilli Powder or Paprika so that it gives red colour to the chicken. You can also add food colour instead
  6. Mix it well, once it is mixed properly. Cover it with a cling film for the mixture to get abosorbed
  7. Take another bowl, mix 1 tablespoon soya sauce, 2 tablespoon Schezwan sauce, 2 tablespoon tomato ketchup, half teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder, 1 teaspoon vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar and 2 to 3 tablespoon water
